Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To foster a community where animal welfare is prioritized, public safety is ensured, and every pet is treated with care and dignity through seamless barangay-level integration.

Our Mission

To provide a secure, accessible, and efficient platform for pet registration and animal control, empowering local officials and pet owners to collaborate effectively in managing pet health records, promoting community safety, and ensuring the well-being of all pets/animals.
